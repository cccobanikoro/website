Join Us

who we are

Welcome to CCC Obanikoro Cathedral, a vibrant community of faith where love, compassion, and service come alive. We're a family of believers united by our passion for Christ and our commitment to spreading His message of hope and redemption. Join us as we journey together, seeking spiritual growth, fellowship, and service to our community, guided by the principles of faith, love, and kindness.

The church choir

The CCC Obanikoro Cathedral choir is a vibrant and dynamic group of singers who come together to worship and praise through music. With a diverse range of voices and talents, the choir brings a unique energy to Sunday services and special events. Their performances are always met with enthusiasm and applause, as they skillfully blend harmonies and rhythms to create a truly uplifting experience. Whether singing traditional hymns or contemporary worship songs, the choir's passion and dedication shine through in every note.

the Sunday school

The CCC Obanikoro Sunday School is an exciting and nurturing environment where children can learn about God's love, grow in their faith, and develop meaningful relationships with their peers and teachers. Join our vibrant Sunday School community! Our interactive lessons, creative activities, and fun games help kids discover God's love and grow in faith. Suitable for kids of all ages, our Sunday School is a fun and engaging way to learn and develop in faith.
