You Belong Here

At C.C.C Obanikoro Cathedral, we believe that everyone deserves to experience the love and grace of God. Our doors are open to people from all walks of life, and we strive to create a welcoming and inclusive environment for all. Whether you're seeking a deeper connection with God, a sense of community, or simply a place to belong, we invite you to join us. Let's worship, grow, and serve together!
