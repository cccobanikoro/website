Mode of Worship and Doctrine of the Church

Our mode of worship is rooted in the Bible and guided by the following principles:

· Celestial church came to earth: 29th September 1947

· Child Naming in the church: Luke 2 vs 21

· People in white: Rev 4 vs 4, Daniel 7 vs 9

· No shoes on: Acts 7 vs 33, Joshua 5 vs 15, Exodus 3 vs 5

· Use of candles: Rev 4 vs 5 (altar), Zechariah 4 vs 4-11

· Celestial Logo: Eye 2nd Chronicles 16 v 9, Job 34 v 1, Crown Rev 2 v 10, Cross Matt 16 v 4, 1st Chronicles 1 v 18, Rainbow Gen 9 v 13

· Use of incense: Rev 5 v 8, Exodus 30 v 8-9

· Halleluyah in the four corners of the earth: Rev 7 v 1, Rev 19 v 1-4

· The gown of C.C.C Worldwide: Rev 7 v 13-17, Dan 7 v 9, Matt 17 v 1-2, Mark 9 v 2-3

· Alter light to burn regularly: Lev 24 v 4, Exo 27 v 20

· New moon service: Num 10 v 10, Psalm 81 v 3, Ezekiel 46 v 1 & 3

· Washing of feet: John 13 v 5, 14 & 15

· Use of Palm frond: Rev 7 v 9, John 12 v 12-13

· No corpse in the church: Ezekiel 43 v 7-9

· Lions of Girdle: Rev 1 v 13, Daniel 10 v 5

· No alcoholic and other intoxicants: Isaiah 5, Lev 10 v 8-9, Gen 9 v 20-25, 1st Cor 3 v 16-17

· Woman mensuration and purification: Lev 12 v 1-6, Lev 15 v 28-30

· Smoking of cigarette and hemp is forbidden

· Women should be moderate in dressing and use of jewelry: 1 Tim 2 v 9

· Use of Perfume: Matt 26 v 6-7

· The long dress: Exo 28 v 42

· Need for Thanks Offering: Exo 34 v 20

· The seven Spirit of God: Rev 4 1-5

· Facing of the East for Prayer: Gen 3 v 24

You Belong Here

At C.C.C Obanikoro Cathedral, we believe that everyone deserves to experience the love and grace of God. Our doors are open to people from all walks of life, and we strive to create a welcoming and inclusive environment for all. Whether you're seeking a deeper connection with God, a sense of community, or simply a place to belong, we invite you to join us. Let's worship, grow, and serve together!
