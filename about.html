Vision and Mission

Under the leadership of our shepherd, we strive to create a welcoming and inclusive community where people from all walks of life can come together to worship, learn, and grow in their faith.

The Celestial Church of Christ (CCC) is a Christian denomination founded by Samuel Bilehou Joseph Oshoffa in 1947. Here are some of the key doctrines and rules of the CCC: Doctrines: 1. *Trinity*: The CCC believes in the Holy Trinity - God the Father, God the Son (Jesus Christ), and God the Holy Spirit. 2. *Bible*: The CCC believes the Bible is the inspired Word of God and the ultimate authority for Christian faith and practice. 3. *Salvation*: The CCC believes salvation comes through faith in Jesus Christ and repentance from sin. 4. *Holy Spirit*: The CCC believes in the baptism of the Holy Spirit and the gifts of the Spirit, including speaking in tongues and healing. Rules: 1. *Dress Code*: CCC members are expected to dress modestly 2. *Fasting*: CCC members are encouraged to fast regularly, especially on Wednesdays and Fridays. 3. *Prayer*: CCC members are expected to pray regularly, especially during designated prayer times. 4. *Tithing*: CCC members are expected to tithe (give 10% of their income) to support the church and its ministries. 5. *Moral Conduct*: CCC members are expected to live a morally upright life, avoiding sin and worldly vices. 6. *Respect for Authority*: CCC members are expected to respect and obey church authority, including pastors and other leaders
