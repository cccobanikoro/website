Brief History of the Church

Celestial Church of Christ is a spiritual, world-wide, united, indivisible Holy Church which came into the world from heaven by DIVINE ORDER on the 29th of September 1947 in Porto Novo, Republic of Benin through the founder of the Church, the Late Reverend, Pastor, Prophet, Founder Samuel Bilehou Joseph Oshoffa. The Church is well known with Parishes, Dioceses all over the world with its International Headquarters in Nigeria.


God called Rev. Pastor, Prophet, Founder, Oshoffa on the day of total Eclipse of the sun in West Africa, when he was in the forest of Dahomey (now Republic of Benin), to purchase Ebony wood being a carpenter and timber trader, just like his father. He was in the wilderness for forty days and forty nights. Just as our Lord Jesus was led by the spirit into the wilderness for forty days and forty nights. St. Matt. 4: 1-11, Mark 1:12-13, Luke 4:1-13. Just as our Lord started his mission of redemption alone, so also did our highly esteemed Rev. Pastor, Founder, S.B.J. Oshoffa started alone, on the 29th September 1947.


The name of the Church "Celestial Church of Christ" was revealed by the Holy Spirit through a Prophet who was held in trance for seven days. There is an order of service laid down in the Celestial Church of Christ for different occasions such as Marriage, Funeral, Naming Ceremony, Mass in Remembrance of the dead, etc. The order for the respective occasion is strictly as revealed by the Holy Spirit and as set out in the booklet titled "Order of Service".

Mode of Worship and Doctrine of the Church

Our mode of worship is rooted in the Bible and guided by the following principles:

  • • Use of candles: Rev 4 vs 5 (altar), Zechariah 4 vs 4-11.
  • • Use of incense: Rev 5 v 8, Exodus 30 v 8-9.
  • • Holy, Holy, Holy, to the Lord God of hosts: Rev 4 vs 8, Isaiah 6 vs 2-3.
  • • Hallelujah in the four corners of the earth: Rev 7 v 1, Rev 19 v 1-4.
  • • Facing of the East for Prayer: Gen 3 v 24.
  • • Bowing down and raising of hands in Worship: Exodus 4 vs 31, Nehemiah 8 vs 6, Rev 4 vs 10.
  • • The starting of prayers by first calling (i) Jehovah: Exodus 6 vs 2-3, (ii) Jesus Christ: Matthew 11 vs 24, (iii) Holy Michael: Daniel 10 vs 12-13.
  • • Female members are forbidden from entering the altar or leading the congregation in worship: 1st Corinthians 14 vs 34, 1st Timothy 2 vs 12.
  • • The position of the Angels in the divine call of C.C.C. Hymn 331.
  • • Need for Thanks Offering: Exo 34 v 20.
  • • Alter light to burn regularly: Lev 24 v 4, Exo 27 v 20.
  • • New moon service: Num 10 v 10, Psalm 81 v 3, Ezekiel 46 v 1 & 3.
  • • Washing of feet: John 13 v 5, 14 & 15.
  • • Use of Perfume: Matt 26 v 6-7.
  • • Black and red apparels are forbidden for Celestians expect work suit.
  • • No alcoholic and other intoxicants: Isaiah 5, Lev 10 v 8-9, Gen 9 v 20-25, 1st Cor 3 v 16-17.
  • • Woman mensuration and purification: Lev 12 v 1-6, Lev 15 v 28-30.
  • • Smoking of cigarette and hemp is forbidden.
