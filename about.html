Dear brothers and sisters in christ, we welcome you to Celestisl Church of Christ Obanikoro Parish.

We are a community of belivers dedicated to spreading the love and word of God. Our church is a place of worship,healing,and spiritual growth. We invite you to join us in our services and experience the power of Gods love.

Rev. S.M OLUWO

Meet Our Shepherd The C.C.C Obanikoro Cathedral Church is blessed to have a dedicated and passionate shepherd in Rev. S.M OLUWO. With a heart for God and a love for His people, our shepherd provides spiritual guidance, support, and leadership to our congregation. About Our Shepherd Rev. S.M OLUWO is a devoted servant of God with many years of experience in ministry. He is known for his inspiring sermons, compassionate counseling, and tireless commitment to spreading the Gospel. Vision and Mission Under the leadership of our shepherd, we strive to create a welcoming and inclusive community where people from all walks of life can come together to worship, learn, and grow in their faith.
