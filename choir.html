Our History

"Our choir was founded in 1979 by a group of passionate musicians who shared a vision for using music to build community and inspire faith."

"Over the years, our choir has grown and evolved, but our commitment to sharing the joy of music has remained strong. From humble beginnings to our current ensemble, our choir has been blessed with talented singers, dedicated leaders, and a supportive community."

Our Mission

· To use music as a powerful tool for spreading love, hope, and inspiration.

· To create a vibrant community of singers and musicians who share a passion for faith, music, and service.

Meet The Team That Makes the Music Happen
