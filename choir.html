CCC OBANIKORO CATHEDRAL CHOIR

Our History

The CCC Obanikoro Cathedral choir was founded in 1979 by a group of passionate musicians who shared a vision for using music to build community and inspire faith. Over the years, our choir has grown and evolved, but our commitment to sharing the joy of music has remained strong. From humble beginnings to our current ensemble, our choir has been blessed with talented singers, dedicated leaders, and a supportive community.

The Team Behind the Music

Our team of dedicated leaders is passionate about creating a vibrant and inclusive community of singers and musicians.
